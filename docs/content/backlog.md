# Tasks

## Sprint 1


* Compulsory task: [assignment A1](assignments)

* Read through all the information on the [course web page](https://www.kth.se/social/group/examensarbete-vid-cs/page/what-is-a-degree-project-30hp-at-csc/)

* Carefully read the [evaluation criteria](https://www.kth.se/social/group/examensarbete-vid-cs/page/grading-criteria-8/). Return to them throughout the degree project.

* If you do an **external degree project**, sign a [**contract**](http://www.nada.kth.se/utbildning/grukth/exjobb/dokument/ExjobbAvtalEng.pdf)
with principal, and <u>if practical</u>, arrange a **meeting** where the supervisor of
the principal, the academic supervisor, possibly the examiner and, of course,
yourself establish that all parties are in agreement on the specification.

* Sign up for [**tutorial**](https://www.kth.se/en/kthb/kurser-och-stod/stod/handledning-i-inform) at the library

* Start to think about your **toolbox** (e.g. reference manager, word processor,
  version controls, graphics software, statistical analysis software, ...)

* Check out relevant [**KTH ARC lunch seminars**](https://www.kth.se/en/student/studentliv/studieresultat/arc-kalendarium)

* Explore and make use of the [resources](https://www.kth.se/en/kthb/kurser-och-stod/stod/handledning-i-inform) at your library
