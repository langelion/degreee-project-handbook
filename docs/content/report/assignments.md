# Assignment A3

## Tasks
Assignment A3 is divided into three independent tasks (A3.0 - A3.2).

***
###**A3.0 - Half-time report**

Required content:

* Table of contents
* Introduction
* Research objectives and question(s)
* Background / Related work
* Related theory
* Methods
* Results

Above content should be distributed according to the standard [core structure](structure.md#core-structure) and further refined by a [detailed structure](structure.md#detailed-structure).

The table of content should be **complete** with all sections that the report
will contain (i.e. main headings and subheadings). Obviously, future changes are
allowed if necessary.

The [methods](methodology.md) and [result](results.md) sections should contain relevant subheadings with the important
concepts in bullet points.

### **A3.1 - Attend two seminars**

It is **compulsory** to attend two master's thesis presentations.

[Available seminars ](http://www.nada.kth.se/utbildning/grukth/exjobb/datalogi/seminarier2018/aktuellt.html)

!!! tip
    If possible, try to attend at least one seminar with your own examiner.

<u>Before presentation</u>

* familiarize yourself with the thesis to be presented (it is posted).
* read through the [opponent record](http://www.nada.kth.se/utbildning/grukth/exjobb/dokument/OppositionEng.pdf) and think about answers to the questions

<u>After presentation</u>

For both presentations, report to academic supervisor (by email, pdf file):

* **General info**: title, date, examiner.
* Briefly describe the **structure of the presentation**. For example,
    * which clear sections / components could be distinguished?
    * did the sections translate to the sections in the written report?
    * how was the time distributed between the different sections?
* Briefly describe the opposition.
    * How many questions?
    * What character had the question? Technical? Conceptual?
    * Were the questions properly answered?
* Briefly describe the examiner's evaluation. What did (s)he focus on? Why?
* List **three good things** that you will keep in mind for your own presentation

**DEADLINE:** As soon as possible.
