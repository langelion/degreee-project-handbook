# Report: Introduction

As the name suggests, this section should provide a general introduction to the
topic and the motivation for the research question.

Important content of the introduction maps to sections in the
project specification. For example,

* [objective](../project-specification/background-objective)
* [research question](../project-specification/research-question)
* [conditions and limitations](../project-specification/schedule)

## Checklist

The introduction

* is written in such a way that non-experts can appreciate the motivation for
the study
* explicitly contains the research questions.
* explains the aim behind the research,
* explains the conditions that surrounds the study and that has shaped the
research question
